    • MINHAZ UL MOSTOFA PRANTO

    Minhaz’s portfolio of works spans a rich spectrum of algorithmic ingenuity and imaginative exploration. From building a Depth-First Search visualizer and analyzing the convergence of the secant method, to crafting poetic reflections like The Algorithm of Emotion and speculative tales such as Secant to the Stars, each piece reflects a fusion of precision and creativity. His technical studies delve into root-finding comparisons, matrix solvers, and graph traversal pseudocode, while his conceptual works—like Code as Philosophy and The Geometry of Thought—bridge logic with introspection. Educational tools such as interactive notebooks, infographics, and video scripts complement experimental projects like algorithmic art and AI-generated poetry. Whether simulating chaotic systems or scripting a debate between human and machine, Minhaz’s 30 works form a cohesive narrative of curiosity, rigor, and the pursuit of meaning through both numbers and nuance.
